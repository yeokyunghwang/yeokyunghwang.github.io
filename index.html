About Me

I'm a Ph.D. candidate in Technology Management, Economics and Policy at Seoul National University, specializing in computational social science and network analysis.

My research focuses on understanding how knowledge networks evolve in AI and technology innovation ecosystems, using advanced computational methods to analyze socio-technical transitions.

I combine network science, natural language processing, and econometric analysis to study how actors, ideas, and innovations interact in complex systems.
