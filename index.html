Yeokyung Hwang

Ph.D. Candidate
Seoul National University
I'm a Ph.D. candidate in Technology Management, Economics and Policy at Seoul National University, specializing in computational social science and innovation dynamics. My research focuses on understanding how knowledge evolves and flows within socio-technical systems, particularly in artificial intelligence, using network science and data science methods. Working primarily with large-scale bibliometric data—patents, scientific publications, and news articles— I apply advanced analytical techniques including complex network analysis, natural language processing, machine learning, and representation learning to investigate knowledge network dynamics and technological transitions.
